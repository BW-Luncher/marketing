Our Mission:

Everyday there are children across the United States that go without a school lunch. We intend to help deliver lunch to every single child that cannot afford one at schools near and far. Proper nutrition is essential for children to grow and learn. We believe that a school should be a place that can guarantee a meal to a child in need. Hopefully, our application will help build better schools, communities, and individuals; one meal at a time.





- the Luncher Team

Our App:

We realize that getting lunches to kids in need is at its core a funding issue. That is why we created Luncher, the app that allows schools to broadcast the needs of their students by declaring an amount of donations that would need to be fulfilled in order to provide lunches for those that go without.
